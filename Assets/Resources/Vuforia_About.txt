This sample app illustrates how to use the Vuforia ImageTargets feature. The app detects certain images and augments a 3D teapot on top of them.  

To use this app:
1. Print the image targets located at developer.vuforia.com/resources/samples/files
2. Point your device at any of the images
3. See the teapot augmented on  top of the image
